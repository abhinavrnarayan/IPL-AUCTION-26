## 8. Extra Rules

- All X-Factor substitutes will receive **+4 points** for being announced and will also earn points based on their match performance as per the scoring system.
- If a player is announced in the starting XI but does not play, **no points** will be awarded.
- The replacement player will earn:
  - Starting points (+4)
  - Performance-based points
- **No points** will be awarded for any actions during a **Super Over**.

---

## Notes

- Bonuses are cumulative unless stated otherwise.
- Points are calculated based on official match data.
